About

I am a master's student at UCAS (2024–2027), working on VLA post-training stability — making vision-language-action policies preserve transferable representation structure during fine-tuning, rather than overfitting the demonstration distribution. I am advised by Prof. Xiangsheng Huang (Xiong'an Institute of Innovation / Hebei Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence; formerly CASIA).

I am interested in deployment-aware embodied AI and validate ideas in both simulation and on real robots.

Research Interests

  • VLA / embodied AI; representation-preserving fine-tuning
  • Representation & manifold learning; residual / representation diagnostics
  • Deployment-aware model adaptation; real-robot (SO-101) evaluation

Publications

FiberTune: Preserving Action-Fiber Visual Residuals in Vision-Language-Action Fine-Tuning
Haihao Lin (first & corresponding author). CoRL 2026 (under review).  [arXiv]  [PDF]  [Project]
A training-time, inference-free objective (action-probe filtering + frozen-teacher residual alignment + effective-rank prior). Positive in all six controlled settings across CALVIN & LIBERO (π0.5, OpenVLA-OFT) and on physical SO-101 (task success 72.7%→78.1%).
