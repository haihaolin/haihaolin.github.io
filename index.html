Publications

FiberTune: Preserving Action-Fiber Visual Residuals in VLA Fine-Tuning
Haihao Lin (first & corresponding author). Manuscript under review.  [arXiv]  [PDF]  [Project]
FiberTune preserves transferable visual structure during VLA fine-tuning without adding inference-time overhead. It improves over task-loss-only fine-tuning across six matched CALVIN / LIBERO settings and on physical SO-101, where task success increased from 72.7% to 78.1%.
